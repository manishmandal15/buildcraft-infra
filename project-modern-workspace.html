PROJECT OVERVIEW

Premium Modern Workspace Design

This project focused on transforming a traditional office into a modern, collaborative and productivity-driven workspace.

The design incorporates open workstations, premium meeting rooms, breakout zones, acoustic solutions and ergonomic furniture to create an inspiring environment for employees.

By combining innovative design with functionality, BuildCraft Infra delivered a workspace that reflects the client's vision and corporate identity.

Project Information

  • Client: TechNova Pvt Ltd
  • Location: Bengaluru, India
  • Category: Interior Design
  • Area: 15,000 Sq Ft
  • Duration: 8 Months
  • Status: Completed
